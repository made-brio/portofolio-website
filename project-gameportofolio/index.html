Hello There!


Greetings and welcome to my portfolio website! My name is I Made Debrio Amarta, and I'm excited to have you here. I am a passionate game developer with a primary focus on programming, although my journey in the world of game development extends beyond code.

With a keen interest in creating immersive digital experiences, I specialize in bringing virtual worlds to life through programming. My expertise lies in crafting engaging gameplay mechanics, optimizing performance, and solving complex technical challenges to deliver seamless gaming experiences.

As a programmer, I also take on the roles of a 3D artist and game designer, frequently using Blender to craft various assets for my projects. Whether it's character models or environmental elements, I relish the creative process of materializing concepts in three-dimensional form. Moreover, I engage extensively in game design using tools like Notion, meticulously mapping out every detail from storylines to user interfaces and sound design. This comprehensive approach ensures a unified vision throughout development, ensuring each aspect enhances the overall gaming experience.

As you explore my portfolio, I invite you to immerse yourself in the projects I've worked on, each a testament to my dedication to the craft of game development. Thank you for visiting, and I hope my work inspires you as much as it has inspired me. Let's embark on this journey together, where creativity knows no bounds, and the possibilities are endless.
